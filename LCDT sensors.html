About LCDT Sensors

LCDT sensors are used to measure linear displacement by converting the mechanical displacement of an object into a corresponding electrical signal. They are widely used in various industries like automation, aerospace, and robotics.

Applications

  • Industrial Automation
  • Aerospace Testing
  • Robotics
  • Automotive Engineering

Key Features

  • High accuracy and precision
  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Wide measurement range
  • Simple integration with systems
